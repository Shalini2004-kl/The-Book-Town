Book Giveaway Rules and FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the rules of the giveaway:

  • Who is eligible to enter?

    Anyone who is at least 18 years old and is a resident of the India is eligible to enter the giveaway.

  • How do I enter the giveaway?

    To enter the giveaway, simply fill out the entry form on our website with your full name and email address.

  • When does the giveaway end?

    The giveaway ends on the last day of the month at 11:59 PM IST.

  • How will the winner be selected?

    The winner will be selected at random from all eligible entries received by the deadline.

  • When will the winner be announced?

    The winner will be announced on the first day of the following month via email.

  • What happens if I win?

    If you win, we will notify you via email and send you a copy of the book via mail within 6-7 business days.

  • Can I enter multiple times?

    No, each person is limited to one entry per giveaway.
